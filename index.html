Space Cadet


A simple p5.js game I created.

Inspired from games such as Galaga and Space Invaders.

Space Cadet is video game in which the user controls a "spaceship" (ellipse) with the use of their mouse.

The goal is for the user to survive as long as they can and achieve a high score by dodging the "alien king's minon's" (rectangles).
